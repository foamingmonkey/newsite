ZOGGS USA

CASE STUDY

Repositioning brand. Resonate with curious athletics.

Repositioning the brand messaging across all platforms to resonate to the core users but also not alienate current casual users. We also wanted to empower the user to not be scared to do triathlons which tend to get a bad rap for being too difficult.

Our approach was to bring a cohesive process to impact customer touchpoints and bring passion to the design of the online and social media experience.

CHALLENGE

Clarify the brand’s positioning without alienating the mother brand

OUTCOME

Bear den helped Zoggs USA reposition their brand. This led to growth in building a more loyal fan base and got people to challenge themselves and go beyond what they thought possible .

Developing the Strategy

I sat down with CEO Johanthan Diaz and the team at Zoggs USA to craft the experience of Zoggs USA to make it different than the parent brand Zoggs UK. In the process, they prioritized the needs and goals of the business and how the US market was different.

Understanding users


Zoggs brand resonates well in the Triathlon circles, around 65%. We felt it was more impactful to get a range of Athletics to try Triathlons to become part of something bigger which was in contrast to the parent brand which was more carefree and simplistic. To understand the needs of these customers, we created unique user profiles to represent the range of athletics.

 

Zoggs Users Are:

  1. Unique to challenges
  2. Competitive spirited
  3. Keen to family values
  4. Enthusiast for swimming

Bridging The Gap


Understanding the parent brand was imperative, we couldn’t change the logo but we could bring a new color palette, new typefaces, and still have unbeaten quality. To be a champion of a Triathlon yet be family friendly for people to love Zoggs, to feel like they can achieve anything. This helped define the position of the organization and created a more cohesive experience not just for the product but physical event and the website.

The Result

An equally relevant website

Changing user experience and Building Trust.

Changing user experience and Building Trust.The goal of the change was to get more customers to fall in love with Zoggs, yet empower them to do more and also have more sales throughout the user journey.

After the repositioning of the brand there was immediate improvement in all areas. Zoggs USA had a 35% increase in brand awareness from all swimmers, 40% new social media, 18% of sales growth despite not having a marketing budget and a dramatic 20% drop in bounce rate on the front page of the website.
